YAGI Workshop is a new kind of entertainment studio for independent artists and brands. Our goal is to return production capabilities — historically monopolised by large studios — to individual creators through AI-native workflows.

We work along three axes: AI Twin, Branding & IP, and Content Production. Each axis is useful on its own; combined, they produce stronger outcomes.

Through this journal we will share our internal process, technical decisions, and the stories of artists we work with.
