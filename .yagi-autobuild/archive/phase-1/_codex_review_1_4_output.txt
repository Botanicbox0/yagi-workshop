[HIGH] src/lib/share/rate-limit.ts:38 — Public rate limit is bypassable and effectively unbounded

[HIGH] src/app/api/share/[token]/approve/route.ts:45 — Anyone with the share link can irreversibly approve and spoof the approver identity
Exploit: The endpoint only checks `share_token` + `share_enabled` + `status='shared'`, then writes `approved_by_email` from request JSON, so any holder of the public link can finalize the board as `ceo@client.com`.
